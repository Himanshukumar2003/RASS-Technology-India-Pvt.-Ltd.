Metal Tube Solutions & Fabricated Pipe Solutions

Our Metal Tube Solutions and Fabricated Pipe Solutions are designed to meet complex routing, strength, and performance requirements across automotive, industrial, and engineering applications. Manufactured using precision bending, forming, and fabrication processes, these solutions ensure accuracy, durability, and consistent quality.

We work with a wide range of tube diameters, wall thicknesses, and materials to deliver both simple and complex fabricated assemblies. Each product is developed to withstand pressure, temperature variation, vibration, and mechanical loads in demanding environments.

With strong in-house capabilities for tube bending, welding, brazing, and finishing, we provide custom fabricated tube and pipe solutions as per customer drawings and application needs. Our solutions are trusted for reliability, fitment accuracy, and long service life.

Fabricated Tubes for Air, Hydraulic & Compressor Applications

(Ø 76 mm)

These fabricated tubes are engineered for efficient air and fluid transfer in hydraulic and compressor systems. Designed to handle pressure fluctuations and continuous operation, they ensure reliable performance.

Precision bending and robust joints provide excellent fitment and leak-free operation. Suitable for industrial machinery, automotive systems, and compressor applications.

Fuel Pipes (Metal + Flexible)

(Ø 4.75 mm – 10 mm)

Fuel Pipes combining metal and flexible sections are designed for safe and efficient fuel delivery. These pipes accommodate movement, vibration, and thermal expansion within fuel systems.

Manufactured with high-quality materials, they ensure resistance to fuel, pressure, and temperature changes. Ideal for automotive fuel line applications.

CAC Tubes & 3-Way Pipes

(Ø 44 mm – 63 mm)

Charge Air Cooler (CAC) tubes and 3-way pipes are designed for efficient airflow management in turbocharged engines, ensuring smooth airflow with minimal pressure loss.

Manufactured for high strength and thermal resistance, these components are widely used in automotive and commercial vehicle air intake systems.

Exhaust Pipes

(Ø 76 mm)

Exhaust Pipes are engineered to withstand high temperatures and continuous exhaust gas flow. They offer excellent resistance to heat, corrosion, and vibration.

Precision fabrication ensures proper fitment, durability, and long service life in automotive and industrial exhaust systems.

Coil Compressor Pipes

(Ø 19 mm)

Coil Compressor Pipes are designed to absorb vibration and thermal expansion in compressor systems. The coiled structure reduces stress on connected components.

Manufactured using high-quality metal tubing, they ensure durability and consistent performance in air conditioning and compressor applications.

Rectangular Structural Pipes & Hybrid Pipes

Rectangular Structural and Hybrid Pipes are designed for applications requiring enhanced strength and structural stability. These pipes support load-bearing and framework requirements.

Hybrid designs combine functionality and strength for specialized applications. Commonly used in automotive structures, industrial frames, and custom engineered systems.
