Infrastructure & Capabilities

Our manufacturing infrastructure is built to support high-precision production, consistent quality, and reliable scalability. Equipped with advanced CNC machinery, automated testing systems, and modern inspection tools, our facility is designed to meet stringent OEM performance and safety standards.

From tube forming and hose assembly to brazing, welding, and final testing, every process is carried out in-house under controlled conditions. Our strong focus on process accuracy, traceability, and quality assurance enables us to deliver components that perform reliably in demanding automotive and industrial applications.

Advanced Manufacturing & Testing Capabilities

Our Machinery & Quality
Assurance Facilities

CNC Pipe Bending Machines (Up to Ø 63 mm)

Our CNC pipe bending machines enable precise and repeatable bending of tubes up to 63 mm diameter.

They support complex geometries and tight tolerances required in automotive and industrial tube assemblies.

CNC & NC Flaring Machines

CNC and NC flaring machines are used to produce consistent, high-quality tube end forms.

They enable reliable connections and leak-free performance in critical fluid systems.

Automatic Leak Testing (Up to 12,000 PSI)

Our automatic leak testing systems verify the integrity of tube and hose assemblies under high pressure.

Testing up to 12,000 PSI ensures zero leakage and operational safety.

Hose Crimping, Cutting & Proof Testing Machines

Dedicated hose crimping and cutting machines ensure precise assembly and secure fittings.

Proof testing validates the strength and pressure capability of each hose assembly.

Induction Brazing & Welding

Induction brazing and welding facilities enable strong, uniform joints with minimal distortion.

Ideal for high-strength and high-temperature applications.

CMM with Scanner

Our Coordinate Measuring Machine (CMM) with scanner ensures precise dimensional inspection.

Ensures compliance with OEM quality requirements.

Millipore Testing Facility

Ensures cleanliness of fluid-carrying components.

Critical for hydraulic and fuel systems.

Tensile Testing & Surface Inspection

Tensile testing validates material strength and mechanical performance.

Surface inspection ensures defect-free finishes.
