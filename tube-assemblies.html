Tube Assemblies

Our Tube Assemblies are precision-engineered solutions designed to meet the demanding requirements of automotive, industrial, hydraulic, and engineering applications. Manufactured using high-quality raw materials and advanced forming processes, our assemblies ensure superior strength, dimensional accuracy, and long-term reliability.

We offer a wide diameter range from 4.75 mm to 80 mm, supporting both low-pressure and high-pressure applications. Each tube assembly is produced with strict quality control, ensuring excellent resistance to vibration, corrosion, temperature variations, and mechanical stress.

With in-house capabilities for tube bending, end forming, welding, brazing, and surface treatment, we deliver customized tube assemblies tailored to customer drawings and specifications. Our solutions are trusted for consistent performance, safety, and durability across critical systems.

Fuel Injection Tubes

Fuel Injection Tubes are engineered for high-pressure fuel delivery systems where precision and reliability are essential. They are designed to handle extreme pressure fluctuations and fuel pulsation without leakage.

Manufactured with tight dimensional tolerances and smooth internal surfaces, these tubes ensure optimal fuel flow and consistent engine performance.

Suitable for diesel and petrol engines, our fuel injection tubes offer long service life even in demanding engine environments.

Hydraulic Tubes

Hydraulic Tubes are designed for efficient fluid transmission in high-pressure hydraulic systems. They offer excellent resistance to pressure, fatigue, and vibration.

Widely used in industrial machinery, construction equipment, and automotive hydraulic applications.

Our hydraulic tubes can be customized to meet specific pressure ratings, bending profiles, and installation requirements.

Brake Tubes

Brake Tubes are safety-critical components designed to deliver reliable braking performance under all operating conditions.

These tubes are corrosion-resistant and capable of handling high hydraulic pressure and temperature variations.

Precision bending and secure end fittings ensure leak-free installation, making them ideal for passenger and commercial vehicles.

Structural Tubes

Structural Tubes are designed for applications requiring high mechanical strength and dimensional stability.

These tubes are commonly used in frames, load-bearing structures, and support systems across automotive and industrial sectors.

We offer customized solutions based on design, thickness, and performance needs.

Stainless Steel Bellows

Stainless Steel Bellows are flexible components designed to absorb vibration, thermal expansion, and mechanical movement in piping systems.

Manufactured from high-grade stainless steel, they offer excellent corrosion resistance and durability.

Commonly used in exhaust systems and vibration-sensitive applications, ensuring long service life and structural integrity.
