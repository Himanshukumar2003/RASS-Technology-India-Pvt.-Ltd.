Over 45 Years of Manufacturing Excellence

RAAS Technology India Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted name in fuel transmission and tubular component manufacturing. Founded as part of the RAAS Group in 1976, we bring decades of engineering expertise, precision manufacturing, and OEM-focused quality to every product we deliver.

About Us

Mission

To manufacture reliable, high-performance fuel transmission and tubular solutions through advanced processes, skilled manpower, and continuous improvement.

Vision

To become a globally recognized supplier of super-quality fuel transmission products and tubular components for diverse applications.

Values

We offer quality without compromise and emphasize process discipline & traceability. Our focus is on long-term OEM partnerships and continuous technology upgradation.
