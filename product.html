Infrastructure & Capabilities

Our manufacturing infrastructure is built to support high-precision production, consistent quality, and reliable scalability. Equipped with advanced CNC machinery, automated testing systems, and modern inspection tools, our facility is designed to meet stringent OEM performance and safety standards. From tube forming and hose assembly to brazing, welding, and final testing, every process is carried out in-house under controlled conditions.

CNC Pipe Bending Machines

Our CNC pipe bending machines enable precise and repeatable bending of tubes up to 63 mm diameter. These machines ensure high dimensional accuracy and smooth bends without deformation.

They support complex geometries and tight tolerances required in automotive and industrial tube assemblies.

Automatic Leak Testing

Our automatic leak testing systems verify the integrity of tube and hose assemblies under high pressure. Testing up to 12,000 PSI ensures zero leakage and operational safety.

This process guarantees reliability in high-pressure hydraulic and fuel applications.

Hose Crimping & Testing

Dedicated hose crimping and cutting machines ensure precise assembly and secure fittings. Proof testing validates the strength and pressure capability of each hose assembly.

This ensures consistent quality, durability, and safe performance in service conditions.

Induction Brazing & Welding

Induction brazing and welding facilities enable strong, uniform, and controlled joints. These processes provide excellent metallurgical bonding with minimal distortion.

Ideal for high-strength and high-temperature applications requiring long-term durability.

CMM with Scanner

Our Coordinate Measuring Machine (CMM) with scanner ensures precise dimensional inspection of components. It enables accurate measurement of complex geometries and profiles.

This ensures compliance with design specifications and OEM quality requirements.

Millipore Testing Facility

The Millipore testing facility ensures cleanliness of fluid-carrying components. This testing verifies that assemblies meet contamination control standards.

It is critical for hydraulic and fuel systems where cleanliness directly impacts performance and lifespan.

Contamination Control

Tensile Testing & Inspection

Tensile testing validates material strength and mechanical performance under load. Surface inspection ensures defect-free finishes and structural integrity.

Together, these checks ensure durability, safety, and consistent product quality.
