Hose Assemblies

Our Hose Assemblies are engineered to deliver reliable fluid transfer solutions across automotive, industrial, and hydraulic applications. Designed for flexibility, pressure resistance, and durability, these assemblies perform consistently even under demanding operating conditions such as high pressure, vibration, and temperature variations.

Manufactured using high-quality hoses and precision-crimped fittings, our hose assemblies ensure leak-free performance and long service life. We offer assemblies in multiple sizes, pressure ratings, and material combinations to suit both low-pressure and high-pressure requirements.

With in-house assembly, testing, and quality control capabilities, we provide custom hose assemblies as per customer drawings and application needs. Each assembly is tested for pressure integrity and dimensional accuracy to ensure safety, performance, and reliability in critical systems.

Hydraulic Hose Assemblies

Hydraulic Hose Assemblies are designed for high-pressure fluid transmission in industrial and mobile hydraulic systems. They provide excellent resistance to pressure surges, vibration, and mechanical stress.

Manufactured using reinforced hoses and precision fittings, they ensure reliable, leak-free, and long-lasting performance.

Ideal for construction equipment, industrial machinery, and automotive hydraulic applications.

Fuel Hose Assemblies

Fuel Hose Assemblies are engineered for safe and efficient fuel transfer in automotive and industrial systems. They are designed to withstand fuel pressure, temperature variations, and chemical exposure.

High-quality materials and secure crimping prevent leakage and fuel permeation while ensuring consistent performance.

Suitable for petrol, diesel, and alternative fuel applications.

Power Steering Hose Assemblies

Power Steering Hose Assemblies are designed to handle high-pressure fluid flow in power steering systems while maintaining excellent flexibility.

Engineered to withstand vibration, heat, and continuous steering movement, they ensure smooth and responsive steering performance.

Provides long-term reliability for automotive steering applications.

Teflon & Nylon Hose Assemblies

Teflon and Nylon Hose Assemblies are ideal for applications requiring chemical resistance, lightweight construction, and precise fluid control.

These hoses offer excellent resistance to corrosion, high temperatures, and aggressive media while maintaining dimensional stability.

Commonly used in automotive, chemical, and industrial applications.

Low-Pressure Flexible Hoses

Low-Pressure Flexible Hoses are designed for applications requiring smooth fluid flow and easy routing at lower pressure levels.

They offer good resistance to wear, vibration, and environmental conditions, making them suitable for long-term use.

Ideal for cooling systems, return lines, ventilation, and auxiliary fluid applications.
