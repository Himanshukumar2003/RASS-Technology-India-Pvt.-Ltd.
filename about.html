About us

RAAS Technology India Pvt. Ltd. is a leading manufacturer of fluid transmission products, hydraulic hose assemblies, and tubular components serving India’s automotive tractor, earthmoving, and construction equipment industries.

With a strong focus on quality, consistency, and customer satisfaction, RAAS has grown into a trusted supplier for automotive, tractor, earthmoving, and construction equipment manufacturers.

Established as part of the RAAS Group, the company combines over four decades of engineering expertise with modern manufacturing practices to deliver reliable, high-quality solutions to OEM customers across the country

Mission

To become a globally recognized supplier of high-quality fuel transmission products and tubular components for diverse industrial applications.

Vision

To become a globally recognized supplier of super-quality fuel transmission products and tubular components for diverse applications.

Values

  • Quality without compromise
  • Process discipline & traceability
  • Long-term OEM partnerships
  • Continuous technology upgradation
