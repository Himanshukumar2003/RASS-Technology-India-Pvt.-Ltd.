About us

RAAS Technology India Pvt. Ltd. is part of the RAAS Group, established in 1976. The group began with manufacturing turned components and later diversified into fuel pipes, hose assemblies, and tubular systems in 1995.

With decades of experience and a strong OEM focus, RAAS has grown into a dependable partner for critical fuel and fluid transmission components.

Mission

To manufacture reliable, high-performance fuel transmission and tubular solutions through advanced processes, skilled manpower, and continuous improvement.

Vision

To become a globally recognized supplier of super-quality fuel transmission products and tubular components for diverse applications.

Values

  • Quality without compromise
  • Process discipline & traceability
  • Long-term OEM partnerships
  • Continuous technology upgradation
